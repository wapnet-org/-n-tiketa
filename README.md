## What is Tiketa?

Tiketa is a public event ticketing platform. Anyone can create an event — free, paid, or private — and start selling tickets in minutes. Attendees can discover events, buy tickets, vote, and leave reviews.

## Features

- **Event creation** — Create and publish events with multiple ticket types and pricing
- **Digital tickets** — QR code tickets delivered instantly by email and in-app
- **Payments** — Secure card payments and Mobile Money support
- **Promo codes** — Offer discounts to your audience
- **Affiliate links** — Let your community promote your events and earn commissions
- **Voting** — Run polls and elections tied to your events
- **Reviews** — Collect feedback and build your reputation as an organizer
- **Analytics** — Track sales, attendance, and revenue in real time
- **Notifications** — Email and push notifications for bookings, reminders, and updates
- **Mobile-first** — Works great on any device, installable as an app

## For Organizers

Create a free account and start selling tickets today. Once your identity is verified, you unlock paid ticketing with automatic payouts and a reduced platform fee.
